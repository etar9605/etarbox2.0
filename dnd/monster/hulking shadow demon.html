Hulking Shadow Demon

Medium aberration, chaotic neutral

Armor Class. 14 (natural armor)

Hit Points. 133 (14d10 + 56)

Speed. 30 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
20 (+5) 9 (–1) 18 (+4) 3 (–4) 8 (–1) 1 (–5)

Saving Throws. Dex +10, Con +10, Int +9, Cha +10

Damage Immunities. acid, poison, psychic, bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks that aren't adamantine

Condition Immunities. charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, petrified

Senses. darkvision 60 ft, passive Perception 9

Languages. understands the languages of its creator but can't speak

Challenge. 6 (2300 XP)

Proficiency Bonus. + 4

Acid Absorption. Whenever the hulking shadow demon is subjected to acid damage, it takes no damage and instead regains a number of hit points equal to the acid damage dealt.

Immutable Form. The hulking shadow demon is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

Magic Resistance. The hulking shadow demon has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Magic Weapons. The hulking shadow demon's weapon attacks are magical.

Actions

Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage. if the demon had advantage on the attack roll, 22 (4d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage.
