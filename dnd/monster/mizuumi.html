Mizuumi

Huge elemental, unaligned

Armor Class. 18

Hit Points. 110 (17d8 + 34)

Speed. 30 ft. Swim 40 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
18 (+4) 14 (+2) 15 (+2) 8 (-1) 10 (+0) 10 (+0)

Saving Throws. Dex +6, Con +7

Skills. Acrobatics +6, History +6, Perception +5

Damage Resistances. acid, bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks

Damage Immunities. poison

Condition Immunities. paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone

Senses. darkvision 60 ft, passive Perception 10

Languages. Common, Aquan, Celestial

Challenge. 7 (2900 XP)

Proficiency Bonus. + 3

Hugging Water. As a bonus action, the mizuumi makes a attack and hit, range 15ft cube, the creature in range was submerged in water (grappled).

Spirit charmed. When any creature comes within 5ft of the edge of the mizuumi, it must wis save, if it fails the creature is charmed and enters the middle of the lake.

Actions

Multiattack. The mizuumi makes three lance attacks.

Freezing Lance. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 20/120 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 4) force damage.

Freezing Strikes (Recharge 6).The mizuumi uses Multiattack. Each attack that hits deals an extra 5 (1d10) cold damage. A target that is hit by one or more of these attacks has its speed reduced by 10 feet until the end of the mizuumi's next turn.

Description

Là một cái hồ nước với nhiều đóm đóm bay trên mặt. Hiện thân là một con cá lớn
