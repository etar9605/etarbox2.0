Dakini

Medium Moon Elf, Neutral

Armor Class. 16

Hit Points. 105 (14d8 + 42)

Speed. 30 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
13 (+1) 14 (+2) 16 (+3) 14 (+2) 12 (+1) 13 (+1)

Saving Throws. Dex +6, Con +7

Skills. Acrobatics +6, History +6, Perception +5

Damage Immunities. radiant, necrotic

Condition Immunities. charmed, exhaustion

Senses. Darkvision 60ft., passive Perception 15

Languages. Common, Elvish

Challenge. 9 (5000 XP)

Proficiency Bonus. + 4

Sense Magic. Dakini senses magic within 120 feet of it at will. This trait otherwise works like the detect magic spell but isn't itself magical

Fey Ancestry. Magic can't put Dakini to sleep.

Magic Weapons. Dakini's weapon attacks are magical.

Blessing Moonlight. In the moonlight, Dakini has advantage when she attacks made by longsword.

Battle experience. Dakini's weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20.

Stone Camouflage. Dakini has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in rocky terrain.

Innate Spellcasting. Dakini's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 16, +7 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

At will: cloud of daggers, prestidigitation, identify

3/day: magic stone, charm person

2/day: encode thoughts

1/day: flesh to stone

Actions

Multiattack. Dakini makes two attacks by longsword.

Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 6) slashing damage, or 13 (2d10 + 6) necrotic damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack.

Dance Blade.If Dakini only makes one attack by longswort and hit, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be Charmed until Dakini or Dakini's companions do anything harmful to it.

Reactions

Shield. An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from magic missile.
