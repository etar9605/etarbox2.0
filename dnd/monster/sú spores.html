Sú Spores

Medium Plant, Any Neutral Alignment

Armor Class. 16 (natural armor)

Hit Points. 133 (14d10 + 56)

Speed. Fly 30 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
7 (–2) 16 (+3) 18 (+4) 11 (+0) 10 (+0) 14 (+2)

Saving Throws. Dex +6, Con +7

Skills. Stealth +6, Animal Handling +3

Damage Vulnerabilities. fire

Damage Resistances. cold, necrotic, poison

Damage Immunities. lightning

Condition Immunities. blinded, charmed, exhaustion

Senses. darkvision 120 ft, blindsight 60 ft, passive Perception 10

Languages. Common, Vegepygmy

Challenge. 5 (1800 XP)

Proficiency Bonus. + 3

Actions

Multiattack. The sus makes three Claw attacks or two Rotting attacks.

Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 2) slashing damage.

Rotting.Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/90 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 4) poison damage with range, or 6 (1d8 + 4) necrotic damage if used with melee attack.

Spores (Recharge 5–6).A 30-foot-radius cloud of toxic spores extends out from the Sus. The spores spread around corners. Each creature in that area that isn't a Plant must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned. While poisoned in this way, a target takes 16 (4d8) poison damage at the start of each of its turns. A target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

The cloud of toxic spores still alive even if Sus die.
